Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how FeedGeni, LLC ("FeedGeni," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit feedgeni.com (the "Site") or use our services. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to improve their performance, and to provide information to the site owners. Similar technologies, such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage, perform comparable functions.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies. Required for the Site and Services to function, such as maintaining your session and securing your account.
  • Preference cookies. Remember your settings and choices to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Analytics cookies. Help us understand how visitors interact with the Site so we can measure and improve performance.
  • Marketing cookies. May be used to deliver and measure the effectiveness of relevant advertising, where applicable.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party service providers, such as analytics and advertising partners, that appear on or operate through our Site. We do not control these cookies, and their use is governed by the third party's own policies.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting them. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality and availability of the Site and Services. To learn how to manage cookies, refer to your browser's help documentation.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Site after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
