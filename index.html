FAQs

How often is the product feed updated?
Product feeds on Paid Plans are automatically updated on a set schedule of every 2-4 hours. You can also manually update your feed at anytime from your dashboard.
What platforms can I distribute my products to?
See our list of feeds for a full breakdown. You can also build your own using XML and CSV feeds for any specific use case for your business.
Is there any setup fee?
No, there are no setup fees. We’re also happy to help you setup your first feed with the purchase of any paid plan.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, we offer a free tier limited to 50 product SKUs and requiring manual feed & product updates for you to test your integration. You can then upgrade to unlock even more SKUs, automatic updates and more.
Do I need technical skills to use your app?
No, our app is user-friendly and requires low technical skills. We provide thorough documentation for each feed. We’re also available via chat and email to help with any customizations or questions you have. Our support is top notch, just look at our reviews.
How long does it take to sync my products from my store?
This depends on your product catalog size and can range from a few minutes to a few hours.
